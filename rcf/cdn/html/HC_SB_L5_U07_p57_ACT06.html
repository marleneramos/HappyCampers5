GRAMMAR PASS

Where were you / they yesterday?

I was at the gym.

I wasn’t at the airport.

They were at the playground.

Were they at home?

Yes, they were. / No, they weren’t.

  1. The sentences talk about

    a. the present.    b. the past.    

  2. The verbs was/wasn’t and were/weren’t are the past form of the verb

    a. to be.    b. to have.
