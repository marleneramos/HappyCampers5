Different Animals!

Animals, animals,

Big and small.

They’re all different,

One and all.

  1. It’s cute. It’s small.

    It can be fat or thin.

    It’s stronger than a frog.

    It isn’t bigger than a dog.

    fox    kangaroo    

  2. It’s big. It’s strong.

    It can be ugly or cute.

    It’s bigger than a bat,

    It isn’t smaller than a cat.

    eagle    crocodile
